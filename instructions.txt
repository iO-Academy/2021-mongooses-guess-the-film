Instructions

Aim: 

Guess as many film titles from the quotes provided within 30 seconds. Each correct guess scores 1 point

How to Play: 

- Press start
- Read the quote displayed and enter your guess
- Click submit
- Pressing hint reveals 3 possibilities and the year the film was released
- Pressing reveal shows the answer - you won't score any points for this film
- To move to the next question, click 'Next'
- Once time expires, your score will display.
- Click Start again for a new game.
